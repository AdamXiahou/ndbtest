MEDIA ARRANGEMENT

Media representatives are invited to cover the Ninth Annual Meeting of the NDB Board of Governors and need to be accredited well in advance. Only accredited media representatives will receive badges for the event. Accredited media representatives should register on site at the registration desk at CTICC II.

The South African Revenue Services (SARS) has advised that media equipment should complete an ATA carnet form to ensure a smooth customs clearance. Any equipment must be declared with customs in advance.

For further queries on media arrangements or media requests, please address to: media@ndb.int
