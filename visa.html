VISA APPLICATION

Delegates and participants who have confirmed their participation to the Ninth Annual Meeting of the NDB Board of Governors and completed the online accreditation will be provided with an invitation letter upon request – choosing “yes” for the visa letter item on the registration page, for the purpose of visa application to enter South Africa. 

All applicants’ passports must be valid for at least 30 days beyond the intended date of departure from South Africa and should contain at least two unused visa pages when presenting the passport for purposes of endorsing a visa. For more information, please visit https://www.dha.gov.za/index.php/applying-for-sa-visa.

Immunization for yellow fever is an entry requirement if the journey starts or entails passing through the yellow fever belt of Africa or South America.  The traveler must complete immunization at least ten days prior to arriving in South Africa and present the yellow fever certificate upon arrival. Please refer to the website www.dha.gov.za and https://www.westerncape.gov.za/service/yellow-fever-certificates for further information.

Kindly note where applicants originate from countries where South Africa has no Mission, the applications should be couriered or posted by registered mail to the Mission in a neighboring country. Please be informed that while some NDB member countries do not require visas to enter South Africa, other member countries are required to comply with all applicable visa procedures. Please avail yourself as to the visa requirements of your home country to enter South Africa. Click here https://www.dha.gov.za/index.php/immigration-services/exempt-countries for country list to which visa exemption applies. Kindly refer to a guide on applying for a South African visa via the below link for additional information: https://www.westerncape.gov.za/service/south-african-visa-requirements-overseas-visitors
