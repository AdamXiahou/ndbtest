MEETING INFORMATION

Venue

The Cape Town International Convention Center (CTICC) is the official venue for the Ninth Annual Meeting of the NDB Board of Governors. All events will be held at the function rooms at CTICC II and the Westin Hotel. Delegates and participants will be required to pass through a security check at the meeting venue entrances.

Address: Corner of Heerengracht & Rua Bartholomeu Dias, Foreshore, Cape Town 8001
Web: https://www.cticc.co.za
Tel: +27 - 21 - 410 5000
Fax: +27 - 21 - 410 5001
Email: info@cticc.co.za
Postal Address: P.O. Box 8120, Roggebaai, Cape Town, 8012, South Africa

Working Language and Interpretation

Meetings will be conducted in English. Simultaneous interpretation in English, Russian, Arabic, Chinese and Portuguese will be provided for the Opening Ceremony, Board of Governors Meeting and the Flagship Governors Seminar.

Accreditation

All delegates and participants (including media) attending the Ninth Annual Meeting of the NDB Board of Governors must be accredited. You will receive a notification email containing an individual confirmation code after your registration is approved. All delegates and participants will be issued non-transferrable ID badges with photo identification. Please kindly note participants are required to pass through security check before entering the meeting area and to visibly display their ID badges at all times while in the meeting area. Passport/South African ID should be presented to registration desk for badge collection at the registration desk at CTICC II.

Registration/Information Desk

A Registration/Information Desk located nearby Hall 7, CTICC II will be available for badge collection and meeting-related enquiries.
